    Scotch Cocktails

    B.A.F.

    Death & Co. Page 204

    Not yet tried

    Glass/Ice

    • Double Rocks Glass
    • No Ice

    Ingredients

    • 1 oz Macallan Fine Oak 10-Year Scotch
    • 1 oz Lustau Oloroso Sherry
    • 1/2 oz Aperol
    • 1/2 oz Gran Classico Bitter
    • 1 Lemon Twist

    Instructions

    1. Stir all the ingredients (except the lemon twist) over ice
    2. Strain into a double rocks glass
    3. Squeeze the lemon twist over the drink and discard

    Banks of Islay

    Death & Co. Page 197

    Not yet tried

    Glass/Ice

    • Coupe Glass
    • No Ice

    Ingredients

    • 6 Fresh Curry Leaves
    • 1 1/2 oz Laphroaig 10-Year Scotch
    • 3/4 oz Lime Juice
    • 1/2 oz Cane Sugar Syrup
    • 1 tsp Grenadine
    • 1 Fresh Curry Leaf

    Instructions

    1. In a shaker, gently muddle the curry leaves
    2. Add the remaining ingredients and shake with ice
    3. Double strain into a coupe
    4. Garnish with the curry leaf

    Eye of the Torino

    Death & Co. Page 200

    Not yet tried

    Glass/Ice

    • Coconut Mug
    • Crushed Ice

    Ingredients

    • 2 oz Bowmore 12-Year Scotch
    • 1/2 oz Cocchi Vermouth di Torino
    • 1/2 oz Pineapple Juice
    • 1/2 oz Lemon Juice
    • 1/2 oz Orgeat
    • 1 tsp Vanilla Syrup
    • 1/2 oz Coco Lopez
    • 1/4 oz Heavy Cream
    • 1 dash Angostura Bitters
    • 1 dash Bittermens Xocolatl Mole Bitters
    • 1 Pineapple Wedge and Cherry Flag

    Instructions

    1. Short shake all the ingredients with 3 ice cubes
    2. Strain into a coconut mug filled with crushed ice
    3. Garnish with the pineapple wedge and cherry flag

    Vejk Sling

    Death & Co. Page 203

    Not yet tried

    Glass/Ice

    • Highball Glass
    • Cubed Ice

    Ingredients

    • 2 oz Compass Box Asyla Scotch
    • 1 oz Chamomile-Infused Bianco Vermouth
    • 1/2 oz Lemon Juice
    • 3/4 oz Simple Syrup
    • 1 dash House Orange Bitters
    • Club Soda
    • 1 Lemon Twist

    Instructions

    1. Shake all the ingredients (except the club soda) with ice
    2. Strain into a highball glass filled with ice cubes
    3. Top with club soda
    4. Garnish with the lemon twist
    5. Serve with a straw
