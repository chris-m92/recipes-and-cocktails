    Enchated Orchard

    Death & Co. Page 210

    Not yet tried

    Glass/Ice

    • Double Rocks Glass
    • 1 Large Ice Cube

    Ingredients

    • 1 1/2 oz Campo de Encanto Acholado Pisco
    • 1/2 oz Busnel VSOP Calvados
    • 1/2 oz Benedictine
    • 1/2 oz Pineapple Juice
    • 1/2 oz Lemon Juice
    • 1/2 oz Acacia Honey Syrup
    • 1 Cinnamon Stick

    Instructions

    1. Shake all the ingredients with ice
    2. Strain into a double rocks glass over 1 large ice cube
    3. Garnish with the cinnamon stick

    Joker's Wild

    Death & Co. Page 211

    Not yet tried

    Glass/Ice

    • Highball Glass
    • Cubed Ice

    Ingredients

    • 1/2 oz La Diablada Pisco
    • 1 1/2 oz Zoco Pacharan Navarro Liqueur
    • 2 dashes Vieux Pontarlier Absinthe
    • 3/4 oz Lemon Juice
    • 1/2 oz Simple Syrup
    • 1/4 oz Vanilla Syrup
    • Club Soda
    • Tin Foam

    Instructions

    1. Dry shake all the ingredients (except the club soda)
    2. Shake again with ice
    3. Strain into a highball glass filled with ice cubes
    4. Top with club soda
    5. Garnish with a spoonful of the foam left in the tin

    Peachy Pachacuti

    Death & Co. Page 214

    Love it!

    Glass/Ice

    • Snifter Glass
    • 1 Large Ice Cube

    Ingredients

    • 3 Ripe Peach Slices
    • 2 oz Macchu Pisco
    • 1/2 oz Lime Juice
    • 1/2 oz Lemon Juice
    • 3/4 oz Acacia Honey Syrup
    • 1 tsp Greek Yogurt

    Instructions

    1. In a shaker, muddle the peach slices
    2. Add the remaining ingredients and shake with ice
    3. Double strain into a snifter over 1 large ice cube

    Pisco Sour

    Death & Co. Page 149

    Love it!

    Glass/Ice

    • Coupe Glass
    • No Ice

    Ingredients

    • 2 oz Campo de Encanto Acholado Pisco
    • 1/2 oz Lime Juice
    • 3/4 oz Simple Syrup
    • 1 Egg White
    • 3 drops Angostura Bitters

    Instructions

    1. Dry shake all the ingredients
    2. Shake again with ice
    3. Double strain into a coupe
    4. Garnish with the bitters

    • No Ice

    Ingredients

    • 1 Grapefruit Twist
    • 2 oz Campo de Encanto Acholado Pisco
    • 1/2 oz Cocchi Americano
    • 1/2 oz Dolin Blanc Vermouth
    • 1 tsp St-Germain
    • 1 dash Bittermens Hopped Grapefruit Bitters

    Instructions

    1. Squeeze the grapefruit twist over a mixing glass and discard
    2. Add the remaining ingredients and stir over ice
    3. Strain into a coupe
