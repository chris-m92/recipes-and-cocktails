    Cobra's Fang

    Death & Co. Page 141

    Not yet tried

    Glass/Ice

    • Tiki Mug
    • Crushed Ice

    Ingredients

    • 1 1/2 oz Appleton Estate Reserve Rum
    • 3/4 oz El Dorado 151 Rum
    • 1 tsp Massenez Creme de Peche Peach Liqueur
    • 2 dashes Vieux Pontarlier Absinthe
    • 3/4 oz Lime Juice
    • 1/2 oz Orange Juice
    • 1/2 oz Passion Fruit Syrup
    • 1/2 oz Cinnamon Bark Syrup
    • 1 tsp Ginger Syrup
    • 1 dash Angostura Bitters
    • 1 Mint Sprig
    • 1 Lime Wheel

    Instructions

    1. Short shake all the ingredients with 3 ice cubes
    2. Strain into a tiki mug filled with crushed ice
    3. Garnish with the mint sprig and lime wheel
    4. Serve with a straw

    Kingston Club

    The Bar Book Page 42

    Not yet tried

    Glass/Ice

    • Collins Glass
    • Collins Ice

    Ingredients

    • 1 1/2 oz Drambuie
    • 1 1/2 oz Pineapple Juice
    • 3/4 oz Lime Juice
    • 1 tsp Fernet-Branca
    • 3 dashes Angostura Bitters
    • 1 oz Soda Water
    • 1 Orange Twist

    Instructions

    1. Combine the ingredients (except the soda water) and shake with ice
    2. Add the soda water to the shaker
    3. Strain into the collins glass filled with ice
    4. Garnish with the orange twist
