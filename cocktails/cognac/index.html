    Banana Cognac

    Death & Co. Page 210

    Not yet tried

    Glass/Ice

    • Pilsner Glass
    • Crushed Ice

    Ingredients

    • 1 oz Pierre Ferrand 1840 Cognac
    • 1/2 oz Avua Amburana Cachaca
    • 3/4 oz Lemon Juice
    • 1/2 oz Orange Juice
    • 3/4 oz Banana Syrup
    • 1/4 oz Orgeat
    • 2 dashes Angostura Bitters
    • 1 Dried Banana Chip

    Instructions

    1. Short shake all the ingredients with 3 ice cubes
    2. Strain into a pilsner glass filled with crushed ice
    3. Garnish with the dried banana chip
    4. Serve with a straw

    Heart-Shaped Box

    Death & Co. Page 211

    Not yet tried

    Glass/Ice

    • Double Rocks Glass
    • Crushed Ice

    Ingredients

    • 1 Strawberry
    • 2 oz Hine H Cognac
    • 3/4 oz St-Germain
    • 3/4 oz Lemon Juice
    • 1/4 oz Cinnamon Bark Syrup
    • 1/2 tsp Aged Balsamic Vinegar
    • 1 dash Angostura Bitters
    • Half a Strawberry

    Instructions

    1. In a shaker, gently muddle the strawberry
    2. Add the remaining ingredients and short shake
    3. Strain into a double rocks glass filled with crushed ice
    4. Garnish with the strawberry half
    5. Serve with a straw

    Les Verts Monts

    Death & Co. Page 217

    Not yet tried

    • No Ice

    Ingredients

    • 1 oz Tariquet VS Classique Bas-Armagnac
    • 3/4 oz Guillon-Painturaud Cognac Grande Champagne VSOP
    • 3/4 oz Busnel VSOP Calvados
    • 3/4 oz Dolin Blanc Vermouth
    • 1/4 oz Alvear Festival Pale Cream Sherry
    • 1/4 oz Maple Syrup
    • 1 Dash Angostura Bitters
    • 1 Lemon Twist
    • 1 Fuji Apple Slice

    Instructions

    1. Stir all the ingredients (except the lemon twist) over ice
    2. Strain into a Nick & Nora glass
    3. Squeeze the lemon twist over the drink and discard
    4. Garnish with the apple slice
