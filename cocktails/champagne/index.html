    Bellini

    The Bar Book Page 49

    Not yet tried

    History

    There is no classic cocktail that takes advantage of fruit puree quite like the Bellini, a concoction of sparkling wine and white peach puree. The drink reflects the same philosophy that Italians have toward food: use only the best ingredients at their peak season, and don't muck them up - let the ingredients speak for themselves. Unlike so many classic cocktails, there is no dispute over who invented the Bellini, though the date is still fuzzy. Giuseppe Cipriani, founder and owner of the fabled Harry's Bar in Venice, adored the delicately fragrant white peaches that were plentiful during the Italian summer. He "Squeezed" one (must have been mighty ripe) and blended the puree with some prosecco. The flavor was like summer in a glass, and the drink had a gorgeous pale pink blush to it, thanks to the little tinge of red flesh that's closest to the pit of a white peach. The drink never left Harry's menu, and in fact he subsequently named a New York restaurant Bellini.

    Glass/Ice

    • Champagne Flute
    • No Ice

    Ingredients

    • 1 1/2 oz White Peach Puree
    • 4 1/2 oz Prosecco

    Instructions

    1. Pour the peach puree in the champagne flute
    2. Top up slowly with the prosecco
    3. Gently stir with a thin spoon or chopstick

    The Bittenbender

    Death & Co. Page 220

    Not yet tried

    Glass/Ice

    • Snifter Glass
    • 1 Large Ice Cube

    Ingredients

    • 4 Raspberries
    • 1 Lemon Twist
    • 1 Orange Twist
    • 2 oz Russell's Reserve 10-Year Bourbon
    • 3/4 oz Cacao Nib-Infused Campari
    • 3/4 oz Lemon Juice
    • 3/4 oz Ginger Syrup
    • 1 oz Dry Champagne
    • 1 Lemon Wheel

    Instructions

    1. In a shaker, muddle the raspberries with the lemon and orange twists
    2. Add the remaining ingredients (except the champagne) and shake with ice
    3. Strain into a snifter over 1 large ice cube
    4. Top with the champagne
    5. Garnish with the lemon wheel

    Plum and Thyme Prosecco Smash

    Source

    My Diary of Us

    Not yet tried

    Glass/Ice

    • Wine Goblet
    • Crushed Ice

    Ingredients

    • 2 Plums
    • 6 Thyme Sprigs
    • Juice from one Lemon
    • 4 tbsp Thyme Simple Syrup
    • 1 bottle Prosecco (Or other bubbly)
    • Plum Slices
    • Thyme Sprigs

    Instructions

    1. Peel and chop 2 plums
    2. Muddle the plums with the thyme simple syrup and lemon juice until all the plum juices have been released
    3. Shake with ice in a cocktail sahker
    4. Pour over crushed ice
    5. Top with Prosecco
    6. Garnish with plum slices and thyme sprigs
