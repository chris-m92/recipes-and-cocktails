    Baba, Let me tell you One Thing...

    Shaker and Spoon - Bourbon

    Love it!

    Glass/Ice

    • Collins Glass
    • Cubed Ice

    Ingredients

    • 2 oz Bourbon
    • 3/4 oz Arabic Tea Syrup
    • 3/4 oz Lemon Juice
    • 2 dashes Jerry Thomas Decanter Bitters
    • 2 oz Ginger Beer
    • 1 Star Anise Pod

    Instructions

    1. Add the bourbon, syrup, lemon juice, and bitters to a shaker. Fill the shaker with ice cubes and shake vigorously until it is ice-cold and shows frost on the outside (15-30 seconds).
    2. Strain the shaker's contents into a collins glass over fresh cubed ice, then top with the ginger beer - we recommend using your bar spoon to force the carbonation all the way down to the bottom of the glass (check out shakerandspoon.com/techniques, "The Humble Bar Spoon," for help with this trick). Place the spoon, standing straight up, down into the center of the glass. Place the mouth of the ginger beer bottle (or the jigger) near the top of the handle right at the edge of the spoon, and pour, letting the ginger beer spiral down the handle all the way to the bottom of the glass
    3. For the finishing touch, float the star anise pod on top of the drink.

    Living Proof

    Shaker and Spoon - Bourbon

    Love it!

    Glass/Ice

    • Double Rocks Glass
    • Cubed Ice

    Ingredients

    • 2 oz Bourbon
    • 1 1/2 tbsps Strawberry Preserves
    • 3/4 oz Lemon Juice
    • 1 tsp Roasted Pumpkin Seed Butter
    • 1 pinch Sea Salt Flakes
    • Half a Strawberry

    Instructions

    1. Add all the ingredients to a shaker with 1-2 ice cubes and whip shake in order to break up the preserves. Then fill up the shaker with ice cubes and shake vigorously until it feels ice-cold and shows frost on the outside (15-30 seconds).
    2. Strain the shaker's contents into a double rocks glass over fresh cubed ice. Feel free to garnish with half a strawberry if you'd like to and have one on hand.

    Matriarch Martini

    Shaker and Spoon - Bourbon

    Love it!

    Glass/Ice

    • Coupe Glass
    • No Ice

    Ingredients

    • 1 1/2 oz Bourbon
    • 3/4 oz Espresso
    • 1 oz Tart Cherry-Vanilla Syrup
    • Dark Chocolate

    Instructions

    1. Add all the liquid ingredients to a shaker. Fill the shaker with ice cubes and shake vigorously until it feels ice-cold and shows frost on the outside (15-30 seconcds).
    2. Double strain the shaker's contents into a chilled coupe (without ice).
    3. For the aromatic finishing touch, finely grate the chocolate over the top of the drink.

    Whiskey Sour

    Death & Co. Page 153

    Love it!

    Glass/Ice

    • Double Rocks Glass
    • 1 Large Ice Cube

    Ingredients

    • 2 oz Buffalo Trace Bourbon
    • 3/4 oz Lemon Juice
    • 3/4 oz Simple Syrup
    • 1 Egg White
    • 1 dash Angostura Bitters
    • 1 Orange crescent and cherry flag

    Instructions

    1. Dry shake all the ingredients
    2. Shake again with ice
    3. Double strain into a double rocks glass over 1 large ice cube
    4. Add a dash of bitters on top
    5. Garnish with the orange and cherry flag
