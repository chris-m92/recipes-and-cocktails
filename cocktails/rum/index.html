    Get Lucky

    Death & Co. Page 171

    Not yet tried

    Glass/Ice

    • Pilsner Glass
    • Crushed Ice

    Ingredients

    • 3 Blackberries
    • 2 oz Flor de Cana Extra-Dry White Rum
    • 3/4 oz Lemon Juice
    • 1/4 oz Ginger Syrup
    • 1/4 oz Orgeat
    • 1/4 oz Acacia Honey Syrup
    • House Peychaud's Bitters

    Instructions

    1. In a pilsner glass, gently muddle the blackberries
    2. Fill the glass with crushed ice
    3. In a shaker, whip the remaining ingredients, shaking with a few pieces of crushed ice just until incorporated
    4. Strain into the glass
    5. Garnish with a thin layer of bitters
    6. Serve with a straw
