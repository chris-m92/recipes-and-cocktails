    Blown Rose

    Death & Co. Page 198

    Not yet tried

    Glass/Ice

    • Coupe Glass
    • No Ice

    Ingredients

    • 3 Fuji Apple Slices
    • 2 oz Chamomile-Infused Old Overholt Rye
    • 1/2 oz Pineapple Juice
    • 1/2 oz Lime Juice
    • 1/2 oz Cinnamon Bark Syrup
    • 1 Lime Wheel

    Instructions

    1. In a shaker, muddle the apple slices
    2. Add the remaining ingredients and shake with ice
    3. Double strain into a coupe
    4. Garnish with the lime wheel

    Monongahela Mule

    Death & Co. Page 201

    Not yet tried

    Glass/Ice

    • Highball Glass
    • Cubed Ice

    Ingredients

    • 4 Raspberries
    • 6 Mint Leaves
    • 2 oz Old Overholt Rye
    • 3/4 oz Lemon Juice
    • 1/2 oz Ginger Syrup
    • 1 Mint Sprig

    Instructions

    1. In a shaker, gently muddle the raspberries and mint
    2. Add the remaining ingredients and shake with ice
    3. Double strain into a highball glass filled with ice cubes
    4. Garnish with the mint sprig
    5. Serve with a straw
