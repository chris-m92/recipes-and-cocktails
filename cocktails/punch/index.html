    Arrack Punch

    Death & Co. Page 232

    Not yet tried

    Ingredients

    • 16 Demerara Sugar Cubes
    • 10 oz Club Soda
    • 6 oz Appleton Estate V/X Rum
    • 3 oz Van OostenBatavia Arrack
    • 1 oz Luxardo Maraschino Liqueur
    • 3 oz Lime Juice
    • 5 dashes Bitter Truth Aromatic Bitters
    • 6 Lime Wheels

    Instructions

    1. In a pitcher, muddle the sugar cubes with 4 oz of the club soda until the sugar is fully broken up
    2. Add the remaining ingredients (except the remaining club soda) and fill the pitcher three-quarters full with ice cubes
    3. Stir until cold, then strain into a punch bowl over 1 large block of ice
    4. Top with the remaining 6 oz of club soda
    5. Garnish with the lime wheels
    6. Serve with a ladle and punch glasses

    Lust For Life Punch

    Death & Co. Page 237

    Not yet tried

    Ingredients

    • 9 Demerara Sugar Cubes
    • 3 Long Orange Twists
    • 3 oz Club Soda
    • 3 oz Lemon Juice
    • 1 1/2 oz Pineapple Juice
    • 6 oz Krogstad Aquavit
    • 3 oz Grapefruit-Infused Punt e Mes
    • 4 1/2 oz Dry Champagne
    • 6 Lemon Wheels

    Instructions

    1. In a pitcher, muddle the sugar cubes with the orange twists
    2. Add the club soda, lemon juice, and pineapple juice and stir until the sugar is dissolved
    3. Add the aquavit and infused vermouth
    4. Fill the pitcher three-quarters full with ice cubes
    5. Stir until cold
    6. Pour the champagne into a punch bowl over 1 large block of ice, then strain the contents of the pitcher over the top
    7. Garnish with the lemon wheels
    8. Serve with a ladle and punch glasses

    P-Five Punch

    Death & Co. Page 238

    Not yet tried

    Ingredients

    • 12 White Sugar Cubes
    • 2 oz Orgeat
    • 4 oz Campo de Encanto Acholado Pisco
    • 2 oz Appleton White Rum
    • 2 oz Old Raj Gin
    • 3 oz Grapefruit Juice
    • 2 oz Lemon Juice
    • 2 oz Becherovka Bitters
    • 6 Lemon Wheels

    Instructions

    1. In a pitcher, muddle the sugar cubes with the orgeat until the sugar is fully broken up
    2. Add the remaining ingredients
    3. Fill the pitcher three-quarters full with ice cubes
    4. Roll the drink into another pitcher and repeat until the drink is very cold and the sugar is completely dissolved
    5. Strain into a punch bowl over 1 large blocck of ice
    6. Garnish with the lemon wheels
    7. Serve with a ladle and punch glasses
