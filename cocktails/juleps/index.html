    Aperitivo Julep

    Death & Co. Page 15, 242

    Not yet tried

    Glass/Ice

    • Julep Tin
    • Crushed Ice

    Ingredients

    • 2 oz Dolin Dry Vermouth
    • 3/4 oz Amaro Ciociaro
    • 1 tsp Massenez Creme de Peche Peach Liqueur
    • 1 Mint Bouquet

    Instructions

    1. Put all the ingredients in a Julep Tin
    2. Fill the tin halfway with crushed ice
    3. Stir with a bar spoon, churning the ice as you go, for about 10 seconds, holding the drink by the rim so the entire tin can eventually frosts up
    4. Add more crushed ice to fill the tin two-thirds full and stir until the tin is completely frosted
    5. Add more ice to form a cone above the rim
    6. Garnish with the mint bouquet in the center of the ice
    7. Serve with a straw

    Double-Barrel Julep

    Death & Co. Page 242

    Not yet tried

    Glass/Ice

    • Julep Tin
    • Crushed Ice

    Ingredients

    • 1 1/2 oz Old Grand-Dad 114 Bourbon
    • 1/2 oz Diplomatico Reserva Exclusiva Rum
    • 1 tsp Massenez Creme de Peche Peach Liqueur
    • 1 tsp Demerara Syrup
    • 1/4 oz Smith & Cross Rum
    • 4 dashes Fee Brothers Whiskey Barrel-Aged Bitters
    • 1 Mint Bouquet

    Instructions

    1. Put the bourbon, Diplomatico rum, peach liqueur, and syrup in a julep tin
    2. Fill the tin halfway with crushed ice
    3. Stir with a teaspoon, churning the ice as you go, for about 10 seconds, holding the tin by the rim so the entire tin can eventually frost up
    4. Add more crushed ice to fill the tin two-thirds full and stir until the tin is completely frosted
    5. Add more ice to form a cone above the rim
    6. Float the Smith & Cross rum over the drink and top with the bitters
    7. Garnish with the mint bouquet in the center of the ice
    8. Serve with a straw

    Matcha Mint Juleps

    Source

    Matcha Mint Juleps

    Glass/Ice

    • Tumbler Glass
    • Cubed Ice

    Ingredients

    • 2 tsp Matcha (food grade is fine)
    • 3/4 cup Cool Water
    • 1/2 cup Lightly packed Fresh Spearmint Leaves, plus a few sprigs for garnish
    • 2 tbsp Organic Blonde Cane Sugar
    • 1-2 tbsp Fresh Lemon Juice
    • 1/2 cup Bourbon
    • Ice
    • 2-3 sprigs Spearmint Leaves

    Instructions

    1. Place the matcha in a bowl, and gradually work in a tbsp of the water drop by drop, stirring constantly, until the matcha is a smooth paste. Gradually stir in the rest of the water.
    2. Place the mint leaves and sugar in a jar or measuring pitcher, and mash with a muddler until the sugar is dissolved and the leaves are bruised.
    3. Stir in 1 tbsp of the lemon juice
    4. Stir in the bourbon and matcha mixture
    5. Strain the mixture through a fine mesh sieve, pressing on the mint to extract the oils
    6. Taste, adding more lemon if it's needed
    7. Pour into two ice-filled tumblers and garnish with mint leaves

    Notes

    1. Ensure the matcha is fresh
    2. If serving over traditional crushed ice, reduce the water in the matcha
    3. The cocktail can be shaken with ice before straining to make extra cold

    Zihuatanejo Julep

    Death & Co. Page 244

    Not yet tried

    Glass/Ice

    • Julep Tin
    • Crushed Ice

    Ingredients

    • 1 Mint Sprig
    • 2 oz El Tesoro Reposado Tequila
    • 1 tsp Demerara Syrup
    • 1/2 oz Del Maguey Chichicapa Mezcal
    • 1 Cinnamon Stick
    • 1 Mint Bouquet

    Instructions

    1. Rub the mint around the inside of a julep tin and discard
    2. Fill the tin with crushed ice and add the tequila and syrup
    3. Swizzle briefly, then top with crushed ice
    4. Float the mezcal on top
    5. Grate the side of a cinnamon stick away from the drink to release its aromas, then place the cinnamon and mint bouquet in the center of the ice
    6. Serve with a straw
